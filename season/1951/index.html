1951 VFL Season

The complete 1951 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Geelongdef. Essendon

Geelong won the 1951 VFL premiership, defeating Essendon in the Grand Final. Geelong also finished the home-and-away season on top of the ladder.

12 teams contested the season. The 4th-placed cut-off sent Geelong, Collingwood, Essendon and Footscray into the finals. Melbourne finished last with 1 win. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
