2018 AFL Season

The complete 2018 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 West Coastdef. Collingwood

West Coast won the 2018 AFL premiership, defeating Collingwood in the Grand Final. Richmond had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Richmond, West Coast, Collingwood, Hawthorn, Melbourne, Sydney, GW Sydney and Geelong into the finals. Carlton finished last with 2 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
