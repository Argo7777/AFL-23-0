2002 AFL Season

The complete 2002 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Brisbane Lionsdef. Collingwood

Brisbane Lions won the 2002 AFL premiership, defeating Collingwood in the Grand Final. Port Adelaide had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

16 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Port Adelaide, Brisbane Lions, Adelaide, Collingwood, Essendon, Melbourne, Kangaroos and West Coast into the finals. Carlton finished last with 3 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
