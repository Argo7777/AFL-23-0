1981 VFL Season

The complete 1981 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Carltondef. Collingwood

Carlton won the 1981 VFL premiership, defeating Collingwood in the Grand Final. Carlton also finished the home-and-away season on top of the ladder.

12 teams contested the season. The 5th-placed cut-off sent Carlton, Collingwood, Geelong, Essendon and Fitzroy into the finals. Melbourne finished last with 1 win. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
