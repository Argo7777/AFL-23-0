1996 AFL Season

The complete 1996 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 North Melbournedef. Sydney

North Melbourne won the 1996 AFL premiership, defeating Sydney in the Grand Final. Sydney had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

16 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Sydney, North Melbourne, Brisbane Bears, West Coast, Carlton, Essendon, Geelong and Hawthorn into the finals. Fitzroy finished last with 1 win. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
