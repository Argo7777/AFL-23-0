2021 AFL Season

The complete 2021 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Melbournedef. Western Bulldogs

Melbourne won the 2021 AFL premiership, defeating Western Bulldogs in the Grand Final. Melbourne also finished the home-and-away season on top of the ladder.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Melbourne, Port Adelaide, Geelong, Brisbane Lions, Western Bulldogs, Sydney, GW Sydney and Essendon into the finals. North Melbourne finished last with 4 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
