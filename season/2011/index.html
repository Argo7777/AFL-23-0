2011 AFL Season

The complete 2011 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Geelongdef. Collingwood

Geelong won the 2011 AFL premiership, defeating Collingwood in the Grand Final. Collingwood had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

17 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Collingwood, Geelong, Hawthorn, West Coast, Carlton, St Kilda, Sydney and Essendon into the finals. Gold Coast finished last with 3 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
