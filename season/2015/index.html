2015 AFL Season

The complete 2015 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Hawthorndef. West Coast

Hawthorn won the 2015 AFL premiership, defeating West Coast in the Grand Final. Fremantle had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Fremantle, West Coast, Hawthorn, Sydney, Richmond, Western Bulldogs, Adelaide and North Melbourne into the finals. Carlton finished last with 4 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
