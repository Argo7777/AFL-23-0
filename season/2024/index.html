Premiers

🏆 Brisbane Lionsdef. Sydney

Brisbane Lions won the 2024 AFL premiership, defeating Sydney in the Grand Final. Sydney had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Sydney, Port Adelaide, Geelong, GW Sydney, Brisbane Lions, Western Bulldogs, Hawthorn and Carlton into the finals. Richmond finished last with 2 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
