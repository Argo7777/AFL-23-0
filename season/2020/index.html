2020 AFL Season

The complete 2020 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Richmonddef. Geelong

Richmond won the 2020 AFL premiership, defeating Geelong in the Grand Final. Port Adelaide had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Port Adelaide, Brisbane Lions, Richmond, Geelong, West Coast, St Kilda, Western Bulldogs and Collingwood into the finals. Adelaide finished last with 3 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
