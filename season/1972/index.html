1972 VFL Season

The complete 1972 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Carltondef. Richmond

Carlton won the 1972 VFL premiership, defeating Richmond in the Grand Final. Carlton also finished the home-and-away season on top of the ladder.

12 teams contested the season. The 5th-placed cut-off sent Carlton, Richmond, Collingwood, St Kilda and Essendon into the finals. North Melbourne finished last with 1 win. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
