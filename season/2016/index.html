2016 AFL Season

The complete 2016 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Western Bulldogsdef. Sydney

Western Bulldogs won the 2016 AFL premiership, defeating Sydney in the Grand Final. Sydney had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Sydney, Geelong, Hawthorn, GW Sydney, Adelaide, West Coast, Western Bulldogs and North Melbourne into the finals. Essendon finished last with 3 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
