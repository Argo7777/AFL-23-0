2012 AFL Season

The complete 2012 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Sydneydef. Hawthorn

Sydney won the 2012 AFL premiership, defeating Hawthorn in the Grand Final. Hawthorn had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Hawthorn, Adelaide, Sydney, Collingwood, West Coast, Geelong, Fremantle and North Melbourne into the finals. GW Sydney finished last with 2 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
