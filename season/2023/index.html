🏆 Collingwooddef. Brisbane Lions

Collingwood won the 2023 AFL premiership, defeating Brisbane Lions in the Grand Final. Collingwood also finished the home-and-away season on top of the ladder.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Collingwood, Brisbane Lions, Port Adelaide, Melbourne, Carlton, St Kilda, GW Sydney and Sydney into the finals. West Coast finished last with 3 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
