2019 AFL Season

The complete 2019 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Richmonddef. Greater Western Sydney

Richmond won the 2019 AFL premiership, defeating Greater Western Sydney in the Grand Final. Geelong had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Geelong, Brisbane Lions, Richmond, Collingwood, West Coast, GW Sydney, Western Bulldogs and Essendon into the finals. Gold Coast finished last with 3 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
