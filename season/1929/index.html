1929 VFL Season

The complete 1929 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Collingwooddef. Richmond

Collingwood won the 1929 VFL premiership, defeating Richmond in the Grand Final. Collingwood also finished the home-and-away season on top of the ladder.

12 teams contested the season. The 4th-placed cut-off sent Collingwood, Carlton, Richmond and St Kilda into the finals. North Melbourne finished last with 1 win. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
