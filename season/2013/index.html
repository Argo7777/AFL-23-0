2013 AFL Season

The complete 2013 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Hawthorndef. Fremantle

Hawthorn won the 2013 AFL premiership, defeating Fremantle in the Grand Final. Hawthorn also finished the home-and-away season on top of the ladder.

18 teams contested the season. The 8th-placed cut-off sent Hawthorn, Geelong, Fremantle, Sydney, Richmond, Collingwood, Essendon and Port Adelaide into the finals. GW Sydney finished last with 1 win. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
