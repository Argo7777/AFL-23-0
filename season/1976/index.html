1976 VFL Season

The complete 1976 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 Hawthorndef. North Melbourne

Hawthorn won the 1976 VFL premiership, defeating North Melbourne in the Grand Final. Carlton had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

12 teams contested the season. The 5th-placed cut-off sent Carlton, Hawthorn, North Melbourne, Geelong and Footscray into the finals. Collingwood finished last with 6 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
