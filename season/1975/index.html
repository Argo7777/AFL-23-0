1975 VFL Season

The complete 1975 season — final ladder, results and finals.

Premiers

🏆 North Melbournedef. Hawthorn

North Melbourne won the 1975 VFL premiership, defeating Hawthorn in the Grand Final. Hawthorn had finished on top of the home-and-away ladder as minor premiers.

12 teams contested the season. The 5th-placed cut-off sent Hawthorn, Carlton, North Melbourne, Richmond and Collingwood into the finals. South Melbourne finished last with 2 wins. The full final ladder and round-by-round results follow.
