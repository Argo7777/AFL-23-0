Richmond — club history & record

Richmond have won 13 VFL/AFL premierships between 1920 and 2020, the most recent in 2020. The club has also finished runner-up 11 times, losing the Grand Final in 1982, 1972, 1944 and 1942 and others.

Across 1908–2026, Richmond have played 2,433 senior matches in the data here for a record of 1220–1187–26 — a 50.7% win rate, with a scoring percentage of 99.7. Their biggest recorded victory is a 168-point win over North Melbourne in 1931.

By our era-fair rating, the greatest Richmond players are led by Stan Judkins (100), Bill Morris (100), Roy Wright (100), Kevin Bartlett (100) and Alex Rance (100).
