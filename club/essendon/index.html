Essendon — club history & record

Essendon have won 15 VFL/AFL premierships between 1901 and 2000, the most recent in 2000. The club has also finished runner-up 14 times, losing the Grand Final in 2001, 1990, 1983 and 1968 and others.

Across 1897–2026, Essendon have played 2,628 senior matches in the data here for a record of 1440–1151–37 — a 55.5% win rate, with a scoring percentage of 106.2. Their biggest recorded victory is a 165-point win over Sydney in 1964.

By our era-fair rating, the greatest Essendon players are led by Ted Kennedy (100), Dick Reynolds (100), John Coleman (100), Graham Moss (100) and Terry Daniher (98).
