Sydney — club history & record

Sydney have won 5 VFL/AFL premierships between 1909 and 2012, the most recent in 2012. The club has also finished runner-up 14 times, losing the Grand Final in 2024, 2022, 2016 and 2014 and others.

Across 1897–2026, Sydney have played 2,608 senior matches in the data here for a record of 1262–1321–25 — a 48.9% win rate, with a scoring percentage of 99.5. Their biggest recorded victory is a 171-point win over St Kilda in 1919.
