Geelong — club history & record

Geelong have won 10 VFL/AFL premierships between 1925 and 2022, the most recent in 2022. The club has also finished runner-up 10 times, losing the Grand Final in 2025, 2020, 2008 and 1995 and others.

Across 1897–2026, Geelong have played 2,617 senior matches in the data here for a record of 1442–1151–24 — a 55.6% win rate, with a scoring percentage of 108.4. Their biggest recorded victory is a 186-point win over Melbourne in 2011.
