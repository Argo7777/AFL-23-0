Western Bulldogs — club history & record

Western Bulldogs have won 2 VFL/AFL premierships between 1954 and 2016, the most recent in 2016. The club has also finished runner-up 2 times, losing the Grand Final in 2021 and 1961.

Across 1925–2026, Western Bulldogs have played 2,116 senior matches in the data here for a record of 973–1121–22 — a 46.5% win rate, with a scoring percentage of 96. Their biggest recorded victory is a 128-point win over North Melbourne in 2021.
